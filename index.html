About me

My name is Mateus dos Santos, I'm 20 years old, I'm currently studying for a bachelor's degree in Computer Science at Euripides University Centre of Marilia (UNIVEM) . I'm living here right now, I'm always looking for new opportunities and experiences that can add to my professional journey. I am passionate about programming and technology in general.
